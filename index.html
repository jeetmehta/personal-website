Diverse Skill Set

As a Mechatronics Engineer, I strongly believe in being multidisciplinary, and having the capability to contribute towards both the hardware and software aspects of a product. After developing skills in both areas through a variety of both personal and professional projects, I now eagerly look forward to applying these skills to opportunities in the sustainable development and IoT world.

Global Perspective

Having been born in Asia, and raised in North America, I've inherently become aware of the various social, cultural and environmental differences that exist across the world. Spending four months in Singapore on an exchange semester further broadened my view, and I've come to the realization that although as people we are very similar regardless of where we come from, the challenges that we face and their existing engineering solutions can differ drastically.

Team Oriented

A heavy importance on sports and team culture in my family throughout my life has shaped the way I communicate and interact with people on a daily basis. Although athletic ability or fitness can vary as you get older, the wide range of leadership, communication and social skills that I've gained playing these sports are completely transferrable, and are skills that I use on a daily basis, both personally and professionally.
